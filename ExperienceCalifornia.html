Experience California

The San Francisco Bay Area is one of the most beautiful, appealing destinations in the world, offering endless opportunities for tourists.

With a comfortable, temperate climate and stunningly beautiful, scenic natural environments, this region should be at the top of any traveler's list.

Come for the badminton, stay for the destination

It truly is a perfect place for recreational badminton players to visit for a spirited tournament and a memorable weekend retreat. When you sign up for the event, you're signing up for more than a badminton tournament: it's a celebration and a rich destination experience.

Celebrate and see it all

The weekend-long event kicks off Saturday with leisurely games of badminton and concludes Sunday with a grand ceremony, a party where we'll celebrate winning players and honor all participants. In between, you can take advantage of any down time by seeing the sights of gorgeous northern California.

One simple, easy travel platform

Conveniently, you can book your hotel stay, car rental, and airport lounge pass all with JOYiZM. Travel pairs well with recreation, which is what our lifestyle events—including Badminton Carnival—are all about.
