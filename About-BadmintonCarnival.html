About

A seasonal event hosted by JOYiZM three times a year in the San Francisco Bay Area, California.

As a recreational badminton tournament, the competition is lighthearted and friendly.

We gather to celebrate with each other, have fun, and create joyful memories.

There are several categories, allowing players to participate in the way that's best for them. These include men's and women's singles and doubles, mixed doubles, and a special 45-plus category.

Prizes are offered to winners in the form of JOYiZM Points. These points can be redeemed on a wide range of wonderful things, from activities and leisure to exciting travel opportunities and quality pro shop merchandise.

Each event concludes with a special ceremony to award winners in each category their prizes and celebrate all participants for their efforts.

JOYiZM invites recreational players all across the world to join us, whether you’re from California, Europe, Asia, Africa, or anywhere. Badminton Carnival is a welcoming, positive event.

Usually held in March, the Spring Badminton Carnival evokes new beginnings and limitless possibilities—in life and in badminton.

Typically held in July, the Summer Badminton Carnival invites players to take advantage of California in all its glory; before or after matches, guests can visit one of the many scenic parks in the Bay Area.

Usually held in December, the Winer Badminton Carnival offers players from other, colder parts of the world a chance to visit a milder climate and add some excitement to their winter schedule.
