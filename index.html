Experience California

The San Francisco Bay Area is a sight to behold. Beautiful, comfortable, and often sunny, this region makes an ideal locale for recreational badminton players visit for a spirited tournament and a memorable weekend retreat.

The weekend-long event kicks off Saturday with leisurely games of badminton and concludes Sunday with a grand ceremony. In between, you can take advantage of any down time by seeing gorgeous northern California.

About

JOYiZM hosts over 600 events a year, from badminton and golf to tennis, music lessons, social mixers, drum circles, and more. Three times a year, we host a badminton carnival tailored to recreational players—fun and social bonding are more important at these carnivals than intense competition.
