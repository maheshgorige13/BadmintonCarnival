The Venue

JOYiZM's Badminton Carnival will be hosted at Synergy Badminton, home of Olympic and World Champion coaches.

The top-grade facility features a large bay of well-lit, extremely clean courts, amply accommodating Badminton Carnival participants.

JOYiZM has hosted events here for many years, and our members consistently provide positive feedback on the quality and cleanliness of the courts, as well as the facility as a whole.

We have literally organized thousands of successful badminton games at Synergy, so we know they're up to the task of hosting Badminton Carnival.
