Corporate Teams in Tournaments

Recreational games such as badminton are an ideal corporate-bonding activity.

Badminton Carnival is even better, because it features a spirit of celebration and requires minimal proficiency.

Friendly camaraderie and social engagement are the primary purpose, and the games will not be intimidating—thanks to our positive, encouraging environment.

The atmosphere we cultivate at Badminton Carnivals is not unlike that of a corporate office. We respect each player's contributions and cheer each other on to success.

If you would like to enter your employees in a JOYiZM Badminton Carnival, we can easily include them in our brackets—depending on registrations, we might sprinkle corporate teams into our existing brackets or create a dedicated category for corporate teams.

JOYiZM organizes nearly a thousand events every year, so if you have something other than badminton in mind—from a golf or tennis outing to a musical event—we can partner with you to customize a special activity to stimulate corporate bonding.
