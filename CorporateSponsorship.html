Sponsorship Opportunities for Corporate Partners

JOYiZM hosts over 600 events a year, from badminton and golf to tennis, music lessons, social mixers, drum circles, and more.

Three times a year, we host a Badminton Carnival tailored to recreational players—fun and social bonding are more important at these carnivals than intense competition.

If such an event is a nice fit for your brand, feel free to reach out about sponsoring the Badminton Carnival. We have multiple tiers of sponsorship available, even going as far as offering naming rights.
